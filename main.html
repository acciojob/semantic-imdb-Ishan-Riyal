Summary

Never Back Down tells the story of Jake Tyler, a troubled teen who moves to a new town and discovers mixed martial arts fighting as a way to find discipline and respect. As he trains and fights, Jake learns about honor, friendship, and standing up for himself against challenges both inside and outside the ring.
