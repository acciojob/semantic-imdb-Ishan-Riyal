Summary

Inception is a mind-bending science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan. It follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets from people’s subconscious while they dream. He is offered a chance to clear his criminal record by performing an impossible task — inception — planting an idea into a person's mind.
